Deus Minimus

You are a god of little consequence. Your people don't know you exist.

Right-click drag or WASD to pan the world.

Scroll wheel to zoom in and out.

Select a Divine Power then left-click the map to use it.

Hunter-gatherers begin scattered across the land. They will form tribes, found settlements, and develop civilizations — or collapse trying.

Your influence is limited. Favor regenerates slowly. Spend it wisely.

⬡ Settlements are founded automatically when tribes grow large enough.
⬡ God powers focused on inspiration and discovery scale better than pure destruction.
⬡ Every run generates a unique world and unique civilization path.
