Deus Minimus

You watch. They live. They find each other.

Right-click drag or WASD to pan the world.

Scroll wheel to zoom in and out.

Click an entity to inspect them.

⬡ Entities wander, gather food, form bonds, and reproduce.
⬡ When enough people cluster together, a settlement forms naturally.
⬡ Once settled and fed, they will begin building roads and rough homes.
⬡ Hover over a settlement marker to see its level and food stores.
⬡ 🔨 spark = entity is working on a building project.
