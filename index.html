Deus Minimus

You watch. They live. You cannot intervene.

Right-click drag or WASD to pan the world.

Scroll wheel to zoom in and out.

Click an entity to inspect them. Click again to deselect.

Hunter-gatherers begin scattered. They will find mates, raise children, form tribes, and build camps — entirely on their own.

⬡ Babies follow their parent and appear smaller until they grow up.
⬡ Animated sparks show gathering and mining actions.
⬡ Entities carry food and resources back to their camp's shared stockpile.
⬡ Well-fed, ambitious tribe members will naturally found new camps.
